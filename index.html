Stores per-session summaries, your best-stroke shape, and what the coach has learned about its own sensitivity (numbers only) on this device. No video, ever.
How GHOST reads you: it auto-picks your more-visible side, measures trunk lean, knee angle and your hip sliding back-and-forth to split each stroke into Drive and Recovery. On Start it spends ~12 strokes learning your normal, then every stroke gets one steady score against your own baseline. It also tunes its own thresholds from your accumulated strokes so it flags only your genuinely off strokes — and softens when it’s unsure it’s reading you cleanly. Calm by default — amber only on a real, repeated drift; red is rare.
